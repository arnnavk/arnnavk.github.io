About Us

B & M Trading is a small locally owned business; our very roots start right here in Orlando, Florida. In early 1995 we decided that we wanted to share our love for die cast vehicles, games and toys. In the 25 years of being in the business that love has only grown, and our reach with it. Now we wish to help you start, or add to, your collection whether you visit us at our store front or order online. We want to make your experience with us to be as relaxing and enjoyable as possible.
We strive to find the best brands and models available for a range of prices, as well as limited editions hoping to help enrich anyone's collections. Our ears are always open to make sure that we have an appropriate selection for your needs.
We thank you for taking the time to visit us, and hope you come around again soon.

Our Mission

We want to give you the highest quality of products and services. We strive to be quick to ship out any ordered items, and if there are ever any issues or comments we would like to hear them. We want to make sure that you are happy with all of your purchases; we strive to have a wide verity of products and try to have affordable prices. Some of the items are rare and as a result they do cost more, but we still try to keep them affordable. If there are any issues with your order, online or in stores, we have a no hassle return policy. If you tell us the issue and we will get it straightened out, either with a replacement or a refund.
